Our Products

Our remarkably fresh products is a result of our support for farms and gardens local to the state of New York. The two business partners we are currently partnered with are Hudson River Rose Garden and Ethan's Flower Field. Hudson River Rose Garden is a magificent garden located in Greene County. As the name suggested, the garder is famous for their rose varieties. Over the years, the owner of Hudson River Rose Garden, Janice Alsteen provided a large variety of roses in different colors to our store, which includes Sugar Moon (white), Princess Charlene de Monaco (coral/pink), Deep Secret (red), Sunny Sky (yellow), Mme Violet (purple), Seventh Heaven (orange), etc. Ethan's Flower Field is another one of our business partner who provided us with a variety of flowers which includes sunflowers, peonies, tulips, lilies, and many other flowers.
